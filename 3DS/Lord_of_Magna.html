Final Festival; Transfering to a new game.

Before heading out to the final festival, determine what you will keep, and sell the rest.

Here's what to sell before the final festival. They cannot be carried over to a new game.
  • Rubber Bullets, Rock

In New Game+ you keep
  • CR (Money)
  • up to a specific number of characters from the previous game. This number depends on how many times you have beaten the game:
    • The first time beating the game, select up to 2 characters.
    • The second time beating it, select up to 3 characters.
    • ...
    • The seventh time beating it, select up to all 8 characters.
    The faerie "winner" from the previous game is automatically chosen, but can be removed.
    • Abilities that require Tension: Flower Storm, Hagelstruam, etc. carry over regardless of the character(s) choosen or not choosen.
    • Any Skill Chips already equiped to the chosen characters also carry over.
  • Pink Fruit, Gold Figure

  • up to 5 items from last game's inventory, not matter the quantity. Valid choices could be:
    • ITEMS
    • Mixtures: Mini, Middling, Mega, Massive and Master.
    • Amelia-made food (Veggie Sandwhich, Fried Chicken, Roast Beef, etc.
    • Bottles: Antidote, Energy Drink, Yggdrasill's Tear, Panacea, etc.
    • Bottles: Fortify, etc.
    • Bottles: Enfeeble, Fear, etc.
    • Flechettes, Soft Points, Armor Piercers
    • Napalm I, Liquid Nitrogen I, Flashbang I, Gravity Bomb I
    • Napalm II, Liquid Nitrogen II, Flashbang II, Gravity Bomb II
    • Salts: Chloride, Epsom, Ferrous, etc.
    SKILL CHIPS
    • All Skill Chips NOT equipped on a character being brought over.
    MATERIALS
    • Kobold's Pitchfork
    • Devil's Hoof and/or Trident
    • Ruby Hairpin
    • Emerald Barrette
    • Eyes (Fenrir's, Garmr's, Luminous)
    • Buttons (Purple, Red, Topaz, Yellow)
    • Beta Engine
    • Cores (Beta, Golem)
    • Masks (Brass, Tin, Aluminum, Titanium)
    • Medals (Saint, Hero)
    • Quartz (Rutile, Rose)
    • Gauntlets (Silver, Gold)
    • Claws (Devil's, Pale)
    • Amplifiers
    • Stones (Fanatic, Lunatic)
    • Bone Necklace and/or Demon Dog Collar
    • Glowstone
    • Brooch (Amethyst, Jade)
    • Magnetite Shard/Ore/Gem
    • Pure/Raw Magnetite
    • Abyss Fragment
    Remember, that's up to 5 items. 99 Mini Mixtures counts as 1 item. A tough decision.
    You should be selling more items and keeping less items.
